About Science, ICT & Robotics Club

Empowering students with cutting-edge technology skills and fostering innovation in the digital age

Our Mission

The Science, ICT & Robotics Club is dedicated to creating a vibrant community where students can explore, learn, and master the latest technologies. We believe in hands-on learning, collaborative projects, and real-world applications that prepare our members for the digital future.

What We Do

  • Workshops on coding, Robotics, etc.
  • Coding Competitions like hackathons.
  • Guest Speakers from tech industries.
  • Project Development (websites, apps, games).
  • Community Engagement (teaching tech skills).
  • Tech Exhibitions to showcase projects.
  • Networking Opportunities with like-minded people.

Our Projects

Some of the Programme done by our club.

Community Awareness

The Science, ICT & Robotics Club organized a brief awareness session on cybercrime, educating students and parents about online threats like scams, hacking, and cyberbullying. The program promoted safe internet use and encouraged responsible digital behavior.

Community Awareness Cyber Crime Science, ICT & Robotics Club

Robotics Workshop

The Science, ICT & Robotics Club organized an Arduino and Practical Robotics Workshop, where students explored robotics through hands-on coding and circuit-building activities. They learned to program sensors and build simple robots, gaining real-world tech skills and teamwork experience.

Robotics Arduino Rasbary PI Workshop

Typing Competition

The Typing Competition is designed to enhance students' typing speed and accuracy. Participants compete in a timed environment to test their keyboarding skills, promoting digital efficiency and encouraging regular typing practice among students.

Typing Competition Speed Accuracy

Guru Samman: Teacher’s Day

The Guru Samman: Teacher’s Day program was a heartfelt event organized jointly by the Shanti Science, ICT & Robotics Club and Bal Club of Shree Shanti Model Secondary School on July 10, 2025 (Shrawan 25, 2081), with the aim of honoring and expressing gratitude to the 250 dedicated teachers who have played a vital role in guiding and shaping the future of students.

Teacher’s Day Teacher’s Respect Celebration With Bal Club

2-Days Robotics Workshop

The 2-day Robotics Workshop by Shanti Science & Robotics Club offered a blend of theory and hands-on learning. On Day 1, participants covered the basics of robotics, components, and programming with Arduino. Day 2 focused on practical skills, where attendees assembled and program robots, applying sensors for automation. Perfect for beginners, this workshop provided materials and a fun introduction to the world of robotics.

Arduino Raspberry Pi Robotics C++

MUN Orentation: Programme Support

The Shanti Science, ICT & Robotics Club provided support for a Model United Nations (MUN) Orientation event. The session aimed to introduce students to the MUN experience, covering key aspects such as conference procedures, diplomacy, and public speaking. While the event itself was organized by another body, the Science, ICT & Robotics Club assisted with technical aspects, ensuring smooth communication and access to online resources. This collaboration helped attendees better understand MUN conventions and prepared them for active participation in future conferences.
